Sparse Grid Finite Difference Codes
===================================

There are already open-source implementations of sparse-grid interpolation toolbox. This toolbox is written to interact with other parts of solving Hamilton-Jacobi-Bellman equation well. Alternately, for more pure use of sparse grids for numerical approximation, refer to:

* `SPGrid++ <http://sgpp.sparsegrids.org/>`_
* `Tasmanian <https://tasmanian.ornl.gov/>`_

For people who are only interested in the mean-field games application or solving Hamilton-Jacobi-Bellman equations (with finite difference method), you can consider my other toolbox

* MFG solver `NOT PUBLIC YET <https://sehyoun.com>`_

which the toolbox references to.
